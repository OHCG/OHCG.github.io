Our Mission

Our mission is to support children, families, and all people in the areas of healthy eating, recreation and education. We offer workshops and education relating to healthy eating options and exercise as well as provide opportunities for self-suffieciency. Our services include educational classes, residential services, as well as a variety of programs seriving the community.
