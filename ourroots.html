Our Roots

Inspiration

Elizabeth Foltenyi, her daughter Nicolette Jenaras and Lake Orion Campus Director Nancy Wurm had a vision for this community garden. Elizabeth unexpectedly passed away in December 2008. We toil, plant, harvest and share in her honor.

Grovecrest
Grovecrest Senior Living facility is one of the many places where food from this garden is donated. Currently, we are also working with them to build raised beds for the seniors to begin growing their own produce!

Selling
We hope to team up with local kids who are interested in selling produce as well as lavendar to help teach them business skills, working together, and so much more!

Lighthouse
Lighthouse food pantry is location where we donate some of our fresh produce so that people are able to come and eat fresh vegetables instead of only eating canned foods.

Salvation Army
The Salvation Army is a great place which houses many people who need help getting back on their feet. We love supporting them in whatever ways we can.
